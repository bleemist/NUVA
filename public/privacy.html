Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026


1. Information We Collect


NUVA Course Guidance System does not store any personal data permanently. When you use our course recommendation tool, the information you enter (gender, education level, subjects, grades) is processed in real-time and stored temporarily in your browser's session storage. This data is automatically cleared when you close your browser.


2. How We Use Your Information


Your information is used solely to:

• Calculate your course eligibility weight
• Match you with appropriate university courses
• Display personalized recommendations

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties.


3. Cookies


NUVA uses session storage (similar to cookies) to temporarily remember your input data while you navigate between pages. No personal information is stored permanently. We do not use tracking cookies.


4. Third-Party Services


We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.


5. Your Rights


You have the right to:
• Use NUVA anonymously without creating an account
• Clear your session data by closing your browser
• Contact us with any privacy concerns
