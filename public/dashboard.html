Understanding Weight Calculation

Ugandan Education System Weight Formula

Total Weight = (Essential Subjects × 3) + (Other Principals × 2) + Subsidiary + O-Level Weight + Gender Bonus

Grade Points

  • A = 6 points
  • B = 5 points
  • C = 4 points
  • D = 3 points
  • E = 2 points
  • O = 1 point (Subsidiary)
  • F = 0 points (Fail)

Multipliers

  • Essential Subjects × 3
  • Other Principals × 2
  • Subsidiary × 1

Bonus Points

  • O-Level Weight: 0-3 points
  • Female Bonus: +1.5 points
  • Subsidiary Pass: +1 point

Example Calculation

Physics (A), Mathematics (B), Technical Drawing (A):

Physics (A): 6 × 3 (Essential) = 18
Mathematics (B): 5 × 3 (Essential) = 15
Technical Drawing (A): 6 × 2 (Relevant) = 12
Subtotal: 18 + 15 + 12 = 45 points
Add O-Level weight (e.g., 2.3) = 47.3 total
