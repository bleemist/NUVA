How NUVA Works

1. Input Your Data

Enter your academic information including:

  • Gender (for female bonus)
  • Education level (A-Level/O-Level)
  • Principal subjects and grades
  • O-Level aggregate weight (0-3)
  • Subsidiary subject status

2. Weight Calculation

Our system calculates your total weight using:

  • Essential subjects: Grade × 3
  • Other principals: Grade × 2
  • Subsidiary: Pass = 1 point
  • General Paper: 1 point
  • O-Level: Your aggregate (0-3)
  • Female bonus: +1.5 points

3. Course Matching

We match you with courses based on:

  • Subject requirements
  • Cut-off points
  • Faculty preferences
  • Your calculated weight
  • Program availability

4. Get Recommendations

Receive personalized course suggestions:

  • Top 10 matching courses
  • Detailed eligibility status
  • Faculty and duration info
  • Match percentage scores
  • Clear cut-off comparisons

Understanding Weight Calculation

Ugandan Education System Weight Formula

Total Weight = (Essential Subjects x 3) + (Other Principals x 2) + Subsidiary + General Paper + O-Level Weight + Gender Bonus

Grade Points

  • A = 6 points
  • B = 5 points
  • C = 4 points
  • D = 3 points
  • E = 2 points
  • F = 1 point
  • O = 0 points

Multipliers

  • Essential Subjects x 3
  • Other Principals x 2
  • Subsidiary x 1
  • General Paper x 1

Bonus Points

  • O-Level Weight: 0-3 points
  • Female Bonus: +1.5 points
  • Subsidiary Pass: +1 point

Example Calculation

Physics (A), Math (B), TD (A):

  • Physics: 6 x 3 = 18
  • Math: 5 x 3 = 15
  • TD: 6 x 2 = 12
  • Total: 45 + bonuses
