About NUVA

NUVA is Uganda's premier course guidance expert system, revolutionizing how A-Level students find their ideal university courses. Born from a vision to simplify the complex university admission process, NUVA combines official UNEB weight calculation formulas with comprehensive course data from all 10 public universities in Uganda.

📖 Our Story

The idea for NUVA was born in 2025 at Kyambogo University, where founder MUYANJA ANDREW noticed the struggle students faced when choosing university courses. Many students didn't know which courses matched their A-Level combinations or how to calculate their admission weight. What started as a simple spreadsheet to help friends quickly grew into a full-fledged guidance system that has now served over 1,000+ students across Uganda.

Today, NUVA is trusted by students, parents, and educators as the go-to resource for course guidance. Our mission is to ensure that every Ugandan student has access to accurate, personalized course recommendations based on the official UNEB system.

⚙️ How NUVA Works

NUVA uses the official Ugandan education weight calculation system to determine your eligibility for various courses:

• Essential Subjects (×3 multiplier) - Subjects directly required for your course
• Other Principals (×2 multiplier) - Additional A-Level subjects
• Subsidiary Subjects (×1 multiplier) - General Paper and other subsidiaries
• O-Level Weight (0-3 points) - Based on your O-Level performance
• Female Bonus (+1.5 points) - Affirmative action for female students

Your total weight is then compared against official cut-off points for courses across all 10 public universities, showing you exactly where you qualify.

🔒 Privacy & Ethics

At NUVA, we take your privacy seriously. We do not store any personal data permanently. All information you enter is processed in real-time and stored only temporarily in your browser. We never sell or share your data. Our commitment is to provide free, accessible guidance to all Ugandan students without compromising their privacy.

💡 Why "NUVA"?

The name "NUVA" is derived from "NOVA" meaning new star, symbolizing the bright future we hope to help students find. The "U" represents Uganda, emphasizing our focus on serving Ugandan students. Together, NUVA represents a new star guiding Ugandan students to their educational destinations.
