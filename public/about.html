About NUVA

NUVA is Uganda's premier course guidance expert system, designed to help A-Level students find the perfect university courses based on their academic performance.

Our Mission

To empower Ugandan students with accurate, personalized course recommendations using the official UNEB weight calculation system. We believe every student deserves to make informed decisions about their academic future.

What We Do

NUVA calculates your eligibility for courses across all 10 public universities in Uganda, including Makerere, Kyambogo, Mbarara University of Science & Technology, and more. Our system uses the official weight calculation formula:

• Essential Subjects: Grade Points × 3
• Other Principals: Grade Points × 2
• Subsidiary: Pass = 1 point
• O-Level Weight: 0-3 points
• Female Bonus: +1.5 points
